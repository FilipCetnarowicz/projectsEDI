My hobby is Scouting.

Description

Scouting is a movement of Young Men who love adventure.
In there in groups we do a lot of physical and charakter challenges.
Everything is driven by our ideas.
They appear often in motion - when we hike, or when we rest by the campfire at the end of the day.

Why do I like this?

  1. First of all - great community. People with values that are able to take up hard challenges without complaining.
  2. Solid environment for self-improving.
  3. Possibility to take responsibility for some local troop with small team in choosen area:
    • "Yellow Branch" - for 9-11 yo
    • "Green Branch" - for 12-17 yo
    • "Red Branch" - for 18+ yo (mostly young guys up to 26 yo)
